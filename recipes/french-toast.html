Vidal's French Toast

Description

This home made french toast is something that has been in my family for a generation.

I use ingrediants that are dear to my heart.

Ingrediants

  • Slices of bread
  • Two eggs
  • 1/3 cup of milk
  • teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add two eggs in a deep and wide enough bowl to lay a slice of bread.
  2. Add milk to bowl
  3. Add cinnamon
  4. Whisk ingrediants together
  5. Heat saute pan to med heat
  6. Once pan is hot add a teaspoon of oil
  7. Dip slice of bread into egg mix.
  8. Make sure both sides of bread is covered in egg mix.
  9. Add slice to the pan and let each side brown.
